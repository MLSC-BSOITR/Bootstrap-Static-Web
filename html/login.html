Our basic thesis for bitcoin is that it is better than gold



The world is awash in cash and cryptocurrencies, but Bitcoin is special,
with the potential for significant price gains, according to a new report from Fidelity.
Bitcoin is a scarce “monetary good,” superior in many ways to other cryptos, gold,
and even government-issued money like the dollar, the firm said.
“We would expect Bitcoin to be a lot higher five to ten years from now".
